Recursion

Subtitle of Blog Post

March 8th, 2015 : 2 minutes

One of computer science's most central ideas is recursion. Recursion is a method in which a function can call itself repeatedly, but finitely.

If you look at all recursive algorithms, they have three parts:

(1) a base case; a final destination; a place to stop.

(2) a step to work towards reaching the base case; final destination.

(3) a recursive call

Consider a simple example about emptying a flowervase that has 5 flowers in it.
